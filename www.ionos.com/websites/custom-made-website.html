Custom-made websites from IONOS — FAQs

It's simple:

  • Outsource your website creation to save time and energy. Focus on your business while we provide a custom-made website service perfect for self-employed, freelancers, small and midsize companies, who need a professional website design but lack the time or personnel.
  • You'll also get professional search engine optimization (SEO) for your website. Your site will not only look good, but also will have the correct metadata, descriptions, and SEO text that will help your site rank on Google.

Fill out our contact form and we'll call back to discuss your needs and wishes. You can also order directly from this website and book an appointment with our design experts yourself.

Not only is your website created by professional web designers, we also advise and support you before, during, and after your website goes live on the internet.

Here's how it works:

  1. When you order from our website, you can directly book a call with us to discuss your wishes. You can also use our contact form to receive a callback.

  2. Afterwards, the design process starts. During this onboarding call we will go through your wishes and preferences.

  3. Based on these ideas, our website team will then create your new website.

  4. As soon as it's ready, we will discuss it with you and make any minor changes and adjustments that may be necessary.

We’ll share the progress of your website by email. This means you’ll always have the latest information, so you can react easily if any action on your part is needed, e.g., like finalizing the website.

Not only will this get your site online as soon as possible, but it’ll also ensure the production process goes smoothly.

Our goal is to create a website that fits your wishes and ideas perfectly. As soon as you tell us that you are happy with how your website looks, we'll help you publish it online and it'll be visible on search engines like Google or Bing.

To keep your website up to date, we'll make regular edits and changes at specific frequencies, depending on the plan you picked.

We make sure the pages of your website are search engine optimized (SEO) so that they can be found by search engines such as Google or Bing. We'll also write web text for you or upload text you've sent us.

We'll present your first draft as soon as possible, and our experts will strive for minimal revisions based on your needs and ideas. Your website's completion time also relies on how promptly you provide essential information and content.

We can repurpose content from your old site and can transfer your domain with a free second domain for a smoother transition. We also provide advice on selecting the best SEO domain name.

After building your professional website, the next phase includes optimizing and updating your site. Regularly updating your content and optimizing your site's text to increase its relevance to search engines and boosts visibility to potential visitors.

We'll guide you through the process. Plus, if you don't have your own imagery, we’ll offer suitable stock images from our vast library that covers various industries.

Once you've our web designers are finished with your website, you gain access to SiteAnalytics. That's where you'll see how many visitors you've had to your website, and how these numbers develop over time.
