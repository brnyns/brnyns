Advantages of AMD servers

More performance

Built with the most demanding IT environments in mind, AMD servers handle changing workloads efficiently. And thanks to the new Zen2 architecture, they provide users with high single-thread and powerful multi-thread performance.

Alongside high-capacity HDD storage, users can choose from SSD and blazing-fast NVMe options. Other advantages include a precision boost and faster CPU clock speeds, and an increase in cache capacity.

More security

AMD processors are designed with the most up-to-date security features to combat advanced threats and side-channel attacks. The servers include an integrated platform security processor (PSP) that verifies firmware against cold boot attacks. With pioneering new technology that helps encrypt the memory and isolate data from different threads within the CPU, your critical business data will be well-protected.
