What are the best SEO tools? 11 tools compared

All you need for search engine optimization (SEO) success are some key data insights and the right tools. SEO tools help your website to rank higher on Google and other search engines so that customers can find you more easily.

What sets the best SEO tools apart from the rest?

The goal of search engine optimization is to improve a website for search engines (mostly Google) so that the content on the website appears as high up as possible in search results (SERPs). To do this successfully, you should rely on data. SEO tools are useful for monitoring your performance and can help you optimize your content. With SEO tools, you can carry out:

  • Keyword research: Which keywords should you create content around?
  • Keyword monitoring: How does your website currently rank for relevant keywords?
  • Backlink analysis: Which external pages link to your content?
  • Page structure analysis: How is your website structured? (Does it generate a sitemap?)
  • Performance analysis: Is your website loading fast enough?
  • Internal link analysis: Are internal links free from errors?

Ahrefs

The tools that Ahrefs offers are aimed at search engine optimization for professionals. With Ahrefs, you get access to the following five tools:

  • Site Explorer: Provides information about the site’s backlink profile and how your pages rank compared to your competitors
  • Keywords Explorer: Helps to find relevant keywords
  • Site Audit: Displays all important SEO data about your pages and checks pages for problems
  • Rank Tracker: Tracks the evolution of your website ranking over time
  • Content Explorer: Finds good content within your sector and helps you to write your own

Ahrefs offers several SEO tools for beginners and there are plenty of free tools available to help with keyword research or small-scale analysis of your website. Don’t expect any detailed reports though. To access some features, you’ll need a subscription. The Ahrefs website boasts plenty of guidance so you can learn some of the finer aspects of search engine optimization.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive suite of tools Can be quite expensive
Some free tools available
Educational materials and tips for beginners

Google Search Console

Google Search Console is Google’s own tool to help with SEO. Every website administrator should be using this tool because submitting a website to Google is usually one of the first steps in SEO. All you need to do is verify that you’re the owner of the domain. The Google console boasts plenty of information and features and can be used free of charge. Google Search Console:

  • Analyzes search queries, impressions and how visitors navigate your site to find content
  • Allows for a manual sitemap to be added
  • Checks your URLs for potential problems
  • Analyzes your website performance based on Core Web Vitals

The Search console can be combined with other Google products such as Google Analytics or Google Ads.

Advantages Disadvantages
Service provided by Google Limited range of features
Can be connected to other Google services
Free
Note

There are many other Google SEO tools you can use to help you improve your search ranking.

Allintitle

Allintitle is a comprehensive suite of SEO tools that you can use to find keywords and identify trending topics and link-building opportunities. One feature that makes this tool stand out from the crowd is its KGR (Keyword Golden Ratio) Checker. This tool takes the number of Google search results that Allintitle finds for a keyword and divides it by the search volume. This allows users to find keywords that don’t have much competition and that their site can rank easily for. With Allintitle’s suite of tools, you can:

  • Find keywords, related terms and common questions associated with keywords
  • Identify current trends
  • Get an in-depth overview of Google search results for a keyword
  • Identify backlink opportunities
  • Get AI-powered assistance with content creation

Allintitle also offers integrations for both Google and Bing. In addition to monthly subscription plans, users can opt to use a pay-as-you-go system, where tokens can be purchased in bulk and according to SEO needs.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive range of tools Free version doesn’t include support and has limited range of features
Integrations with Google Search Console/Bing Webmaster Tools
Option to export results in a CSV file

Ryte

Much like Ahrefs, Ryte is an extensive SEO suite that is used to optimize search engine performance. Its target market is predominantly professional marketers and it offers support in the following areas:

  • User experience: Which steps can you take to enhance user experience on your site?
  • SEO: What data is SEO-relevant?
  • Quality control: Does your website contain errors?
  • Web performance: Are there technical improvements that can be made to your website?
  • Sustainability: Are you using a sustainable website design?
  • Accessibility: Is your website accessible?
  • Compliance: Are your privacy settings GDPR-compliant?

Ryte stands out with its reports on sustainability, accessibility and privacy, which aren’t typically included in many of the other SEO tools available on the market. Ryte also offers content creation tools, which provide tips on word frequency to make texts more suitable for search engines.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive suite Fairly expensive
Tips for content creation
Reports on sustainability, accessibility and privacy

Screaming Frog

SEO agency Screaming Frog has two popular tools that come in a free and a paid version.

  • SEO Spider: The crawler checks your website for errors and duplicate content. In addition, the software creates an XML sitemap. The paid-for version boasts additional analytics tools and plenty of resources. Google services can also be added.
  • Log File Analyser: This tool provides interesting data on how search engine crawlers perceive your website. This way you can explore any potential issues in your page structure. The premium version comes with technical support and places no limits on projects or log events.

Both SEO tools need to be downloaded and are available for Windows, Mac and Linux. The free options also include all the most important features.

Advantages Disadvantages
Most important features available in free version Limited amount of features
Requires program installation

Conductor (formerly Searchmetrics)

The Conductor platform combines a comprehensive range of useful tools:

  • Keyword research and tracking: Extensive competitive analysis and identification of potential keywords
  • Content experience: Assistance with content creation including integration of a task manager
  • Performance measurement: Rankings and different options for reporting
  • Site health optimization: Review of the technical implementation of your website

The Conductor suite is a paid service and prices are disclosed upon request. Just like Ahrefs, Conductor provides tons of information, guidance and tutorials on its website to help beginners get started with SEO.

Advantages Disadvantages
Comprehensive platform Prices available upon request
Educational materials and tips for SEO beginners

Semrush

Semrush offers more than 50 different tools. Some of them are similar, but each focuses on a different aspect of SEO. For example, there are six different tools for keyword research. Broadly speaking, the different tools can be grouped into the following categories:

  • SEO: Backlink analysis, keyword research, ranking analyses, etc.
  • Content: Help with content creation, marketing calendar, etc.
  • Advertising: Support with online advertising
  • Social Media: Scheduling posts, monitoring competition, organizing Facebook Ads
  • Ad analysis: SEO competitor reports

There are three different plans that Semrush offers, each offering a different constellation of SEO tools, features and training material. However, prices are fairly steep. A free option is available that includes some of the tools, but it has limited functionality.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive suite with over 50 SEO Tools Very expensive
Educational resources, webinars and tips for beginners Can be too expensive for freelancers and companies with smaller budgets
Free version available with limited range of features Business plan has low usage limits

Seobility

Seobility combines plenty of useful features but overall is a little leaner in its breadth of functionality compared to competitors. The main areas of support include:

  • On-page optimization: Entire website is searched to reveal structures and identify errors
  • Rank tracking: Displays how your website is positioned in search engines
  • Backlink analyses: Determines the backlink profile and provides tips for new links from external websites

Seobility’s basic plan can be used free of charge. This is a great option for website owners who prefer to take care of search engine optimization themselves. The free version combines the most important features, but only allows for a single domain to be optimized. For additional capacity, a comparatively inexpensive premium version is available.

What’s more, Seobility provides a few smaller tools on its website that are freely available and do not require users to sign up. Included in these are a keyword checker and a WDF*IDF tool.

Advantages Disadvantages
Free version includes essential features Comparatively limited functionality
Can be used without needing to sign up No instructional content for beginners
Can be used for free with certain restrictions Sometimes lacks flexibility in reporting options

Sistrix

Sistrix is well known for its visibility index. The SEO tool can be used for other tasks as well, with a special focus on the following:

  • Visibility index: Checks your website visibility on Google
  • Competitor Analysis: Checks the visibility of your competitors
  • Content Assistant: Helps you to write SEO-optimized texts
  • On-page Audit: Spots errors and identifies areas for optimization

Even though the visibility index is the most interesting feature (in the context of competitive analysis), the provider also offers tools for keyword research, backlink analysis and Google Ads.

Given its wide range of offers, Sistrix is aimed at SEO professionals, which is why prices are higher. Much like other providers of extensive SEO suites, Sistrix offers plenty of information and resources on its website.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive suites Quite expensive
Industry-relevant visibility UI somewhat complicated
Educational content and tips for beginners

XOVI

XOVI is an all-round solution. The suite bundles eight different tools:

  • Keywords: Helps with the development of keyword strategies
  • Monitoring: Monitors website ranking for certain keywords
  • On-page: Spots errors in website structure
  • Links: Assembles a backlink profile
  • Disavow: Ensures your website doesn’t get Google penalties
  • Search Analytics: Analyzes how visitors arrive on your website
  • SEA: Supports Google Ads
  • Reporting: Lets you assemble different reports

XOVI is aimed at both website administrators and SEO professionals. Different pricing plans are available. These differ predominantly in the number of monitorable URLs and user accounts you can have. XOVI also offers learning materials and organizes educational seminars.

Advantages Disadvantages
Extensive suite Fairly expensive
Learning resources and tips for beginners

Yoast

Yoast is a little different. With the popular SEO plugin for WordPress, you can work directly in the dashboard of the content management system (CMS), allowing you to implement changes immediately. The extension can:

  • Help you create meta data
  • Provide SEO content writing support
  • Automatically create structured data
  • Optimize page load speed
  • Create XLM sitemaps

The free version is a good start, but for additional support with content creation, help with internal links and professional support, you’ll need the premium plan.

Advantages Disadvantages
Basic features available for free Only suitable for WordPress websites
Works directly in the WordPress dashboard Limited functionality
Summary

Most of the SEO tools on our list are all-in-one solutions. This means you can use them for every aspect of search engine optimization. To this end, they’re good for creating reports that monitor website performance over a long period of time. But quick insights can also be just as useful. SEO plugins for browsers display plenty of information about your website while you’re surfing it.
