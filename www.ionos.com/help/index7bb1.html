Scope of Support for Server Products

Please use the “Print” function at the bottom of the page to create a PDF.

Our goal is to ensure the reliable operation of your server, as well as provide assistance with all IONOS interfaces, products, and related questions.

For all IONOS Server products, we support the IONOS user interfaces, the server hardware, the IONOS network, and any software included with base installations.

Please contact support to request assistance. You can reach us at 1-877-206-4255. The representative will determine the feasibility of your request and provide a cost estimate for services outside the scope of support (if applicable).

Paid Administrative Services

Paid administrative services go beyond the scope of regular support. Typical tasks include (but are not limited to):

  • Firewall configuration
  • Installing and updating operating systems
  • Server migration
  • Security settings

You can reach us at 1-877-206-4255. The representative will determine the feasibility of your request and provide a quote (if applicable).

We base the decision to accommodate a request that is outside the scope of regular support on:

  • The issue's complexity
  • The urgency of the task
  • The availability of qualified support staff
