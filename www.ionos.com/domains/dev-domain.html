Why register a .dev domain?

Let them know

There are so many domain extensions to choose from, but .dev is unique. It's a space devoted to developers. People immediately know what to expect when visiting a .dev website.

Show the world

You are proud of your accomplishments – and rightly so. Connect with potential clients or employers by showcasing your best work or sharing your favorite projects with others.

Get built-in security

The .dev top-level domain is included on the HSTS preload list, making HTTPS a requirement for all connections to .dev websites without needing individual HSTS registration or configuration.

Frequently asked questions

The .DEV TLD is a secure namespace, meaning that HTTPS is required for all .DEV websites. You can register your .DEV domain name now, but in order for it to work properly in browsers, you must first configure HTTPS serving by configuring an SSL Certificate with your domain name. For each individual domain contract you'll receive a free SSL certificate, which can be easily set up within your control panel after successful domain registration.
